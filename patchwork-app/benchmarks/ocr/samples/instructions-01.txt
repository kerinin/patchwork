HOW TO DEVELOP BLACK AND WHITE FILM
====================================

MATERIALS NEEDED:
  - Developing tank and reel
  - Developer (D-76 recommended)
  - Stop bath
  - Fixer
  - Thermometer
  - Timer
  - Film clips
  - Dark bag or darkroom

PROCEDURE:

Step 1 -- Load the film
	In complete darkness, open the film canister
	and wind the film onto the reel. Place in
	tank and secure lid.

Step 2 -- Mix chemicals
	Developer:  68 degrees F (20 C)
	Stop bath:  65-70 degrees F
	Fixer:      65-70 degrees F

Step 3 -- Develop
	Pour developer into tank. Start timer.
	Agitate for first 30 seconds, then 5 seconds
	every 30 seconds. Total time: 7-9 minutes
	(check film data sheet).

Step 4 -- Stop and fix
	Pour out developer. Add stop bath for 30 sec.
	Pour out. Add fixer for 5 minutes.

Step 5 -- Wash and dry
	Running water wash: 10 minutes minimum.
	Hang to dry in dust-free area.

CAUTION: Chemicals can stain. Work carefully.
