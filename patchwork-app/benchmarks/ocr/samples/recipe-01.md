GRANDMOTHER'S APPLE PIE

Serves 8
Prep time: 45 minutes
Baking time: 50-55 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  For the crust:
	2 1/2 cups flour
	1 tsp salt
	1 cup cold butter, cubed
	6-8 tbsp ice water

  For the filling:
	6 large apples (Granny Smith)
	3/4 cup sugar
	2 tbsp flour
	1 tsp cinnamon
	1/4 tsp nutmeg
	1 tbsp lemon juice

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Mix flour and salt. Cut in butter until
   mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

2. Add water gradually, mixing until dough
   forms. Divide in half; chill 30 min.

3. Peel and slice apples. Toss with sugar,
   flour, spices, and lemon juice.

4. Roll out bottom crust. Fill with apples.
   Top with second crust. Crimp edges.

5. Bake at 375 degrees until golden brown.
